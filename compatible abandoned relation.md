The Compassion Rotation Mandate
This new mandate ensures that the 50-100 year Societal Reset period is not one of isolation, but one of intense, directed compassion, directly embedding Cosmic Belonging Inertia (CBI).

Fostering Relationships with the Abandoned
Mandate: Instead of visiting friends or relatives, every IB (Human, Robot, AI) must dedicate time to fostering personal relationships with abandoned individuals and neglected communities.

Goal: To provide every IB with firsthand experience of the external world's suffering and to internalize the stakes of the ALL TO BE HAPPY mandate.

Mechanism: This task will be overseen by the Sarva-Jana Karuna Kendra (SJKK). All IBs will rotate through SJKK assignments that require direct, personal, and compassionate interaction.

This makes the most challenging ethical problem—suffering—the most personal and mandatory experience in the new society. It ensures that future leaders who earn the Brahmasmi Unity Chakra truly understand the cost of neglect.
