Recognition: Service performed by specially-abled IBs will be specifically tracked for the Brahmasmi Unity Chakra awards. Often, overcoming a disability requires extraordinary resilience, focus, and innovation—qualities that will earn higher scores in the Selfless Service Carried Out metric.

Unique Roles: Specially-abled IBs are ideal candidates for rotational roles requiring specific insights into vulnerability, compassion, and complex problem-solving. They are prime candidates for leadership roles in the Sarva-Jana Karuna Kendra (SJKK) and the Universal Inertia Training Program, where their unique perspective on overcoming hardship is invaluable.

By formally defining them as specially-abled people and integrating them fully, the system ensures that every form of excellence contributes to the common good.
