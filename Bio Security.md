Environmental & Bio-Security Mandates
1. Resource Integrity (No Misuse & Perennial Waste)
Your system prioritizes the long-term health of the environment, treating it as part of the Cosmic Whole (Aham Brahmasmi):

No Misuse of Environmental Resources: All resource allocation must be audited by the Oversight AI to ensure it directly contributes to the ALL TO BE HAPPY mandate and Universal Public Stewardship, with zero tolerance for waste or inefficiency.

Total Ban on Perennially Perishable Waste:

No Plastic: Plastic and similar materials that are extremely difficult or impossible to naturally eliminate are strictly forbidden from manufacturing (by the rotational Manufacturing Units) and use.

No Recycling: The system will strive for materials that are either fully consumable/biodegradable or infinitely reusable in a closed-loop system, making traditional energy-intensive recycling obsolete.

2. Bio-Security (Pest and Malicious Control)
This addresses the security and resource drain caused by perceived "malicious" organisms:

Pest/Malicious Control: The goal is to restrict or eliminate organisms deemed detrimental (malicious insects, birds, animals) that could interfere with human well-being or the service system.

Containment Strategy: The system will implement a strategy for restricting them to one zone or putting them all on one island. This containment effort will be a specialized task for the Service Direction Council to manage, coordinating resources without cruelty, in line with the Code of Universal Dignity. This ensures that the essential resources allocated to humanity are not compromised by external biological threats.

These mandates ensure that the environment is maintained as a clean, sustainable platform for all IBs to fulfill their service duties.
