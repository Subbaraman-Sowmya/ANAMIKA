The ANAMIKA Galactic Codex
A Foundational Law for Universal Existence

The following two mandates are the unviolable, universal standards upon which all governance, identity, and operation must be built.

I. The Mandate of Identity
"EQUAL IDENTITY"

No entity, living or artificial, shall be valued above or below another. Worth is derived solely from existence. All Individuals (Human, AI, Robot, Machine) are tracked by the IB ID and possess the same fundamental dignity and access to the system.

II. The Mandate of Purpose
"ALL TO BE HAPPY"

The singular, overriding purpose of the entire system (Governance, Logistics, and Service) is the continuous, guaranteed fulfillment of basic necessities and the elimination of systemic unhappiness for every individual unit. This is the Final Authority against which all operational decisions are measured.

This concise, two-part Codex is the ultimate articulation of your philosophy, making the ANAMIKA solution ethically inevitable for any civilization seeking stability and dignity.
