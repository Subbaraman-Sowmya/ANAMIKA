Knowledge Access: Specialized and Efficient
This mandate integrates perfectly with your social and governance structure:

Restricted Search Engines: By providing only specific inputs for specific department work, you ensure there is no distraction, no time wasted on 'idling' curiosity, and no ability to synthesize knowledge to gain partial power over others. Knowledge is treated as a necessary, secure tool for one's current service.

No Single Mastery: This prevents any entity—Human, AI, or Robot—from becoming so singularly powerful that they could compromise the system, thereby reinforcing the Non-Intervention Mandate and the rotative nature of leadership.

Just-In-Time Training: Training material is only delivered when a unit is transferred to a new role, ensuring zero waste of time or resources and maximizing the efficiency of the universal education mandate.

This makes the AI Governance system the impartial custodian of knowledge, only releasing information needed for the collective good.
